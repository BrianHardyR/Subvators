Positioning guardrail:
Subvators Hub is a Kenya-based catalytic grassroots innovation and partnership organisation. It is not an app, SaaS product or software platform. Technology, AI-enabled tools and evidence systems can appear as supporting capabilities, but the public website should present Subvators as a bridge-builder, strategic advisor and ecosystem convener for community-rooted organisations.

Core idea:
Subvators Hub makes grassroots innovation visible, credible and fundable.

## Field Atlas Direction

The approved visual direction is Field Atlas: a framed editorial canvas, asymmetric proof object, vertical section rail, oversized headline, numbered partner ledger and controlled negative space. The Japanese inspiration should inform topology only. Do not copy Japanese motifs, ornaments or cultural cues.

Design principles:
- Use the framed canvas as the primary website surface.
- Keep the first view partner-facing, institutional and direct.
- Treat images as evidence objects, not decoration.
- Use numbered rows and ledger structures instead of generic card grids.
- Use green as the institutional anchor and burgundy as the high-emphasis health, protection and advisory color.
- Keep technology secondary to mission and partnership.

Recommended navigation:
- Field
- Ledger
- Evidence
- Advisory
- Partner

The visible section counter can stay as 01/05 through 05/05. Additional content such as organisation overview, why it matters and founder content should be nested inside the main Field Atlas flow instead of adding more navigation items.

## Homepage Flow

### 01 Field Hero And Organisation Position

Purpose:
Establish identity, mission, credibility and the correct organisational category.

Short organisation statement:
Subvators Hub is a Kenya-based catalytic organisation dedicated to amplifying grassroots voices and influencing socio-economic reform through practical innovation. It works as a bridge between communities, policymakers, strategic partners, donors and global actors so locally led solutions can shape wider systems change.

Health and wellbeing emphasis:
The site should frame health broadly: better nutrition, safer environments, stronger household resilience, women's participation, youth opportunity and community-rooted protection pathways.

### 02 Terrain And Operating Layer

Purpose:
Explain the problem context and how Subvators moves local promise into partner-ready action.

Section label:
Operating layer

Headline:
From community promise to system-level impact.

Intro:
Subvators works across the practical steps that turn local promise into partner-ready action. The Hub does not replace local leadership. It strengthens the systems around it.

Why it matters content:
Kenya continues to face socio-economic barriers that directly affect health and wellbeing. Youth unemployment constrains stable livelihoods. Gender inequality limits women's full participation in economic and public life. Weak citizen engagement often leaves grassroots groups outside reforms that shape food systems, public health, safety and opportunity.

At community level, these pressures show up in poor diets, unsafe migration, inadequate sanitation, environmental stress, weak referral systems and limited institutional capacity among community-rooted actors. Subvators works in this gap by helping local organisations become visible, credible, fundable and influential.

Operating steps:

    01 Observe    Field evidence and community realities
    02 Strengthen Institutional development
    03 Package    Concepts, decks and donor readiness
    04 Connect    Strategic partnerships
    05 Scale      Evidence-led learning and reform

Step copy:
- Observe: We listen to local actors, identify practical community priorities and surface the evidence that institutions often miss.
- Strengthen: We support governance, finance, compliance, fundraising readiness and organisational positioning so grassroots partners can grow with confidence.
- Package: We help translate local ideas into clear collaboration models, donor concepts, budgets, narratives and partner-ready cases.
- Connect: We connect community-rooted organisations with donors, universities, policymakers, diaspora networks and global actors.
- Scale: We support research, dashboards, workflow tools and feedback loops that help partners improve decisions and demonstrate impact.

Layout notes:
- Use five numbered panels or a horizontal board, not cards inside cards.
- The why it matters copy can sit as a terrain note beside the operating model or as a short full-width band before it.
- Keep copy compact in the live layout; the full wording is available for secondary pages or expanded states.
